About me

Hi I'm Oliver. I'm a solo developer working on easi-type, I built easi-type because I was consistantly sick of never being able to practice touch typing in a clean, and customizable way. Currrently, easi-type is still in a embryonic stage of development and there's a lot of features soon to be released.

What is touch typing ?

Typing is an important skill to have mastered in this day and age, as computers, computing and technology in general increase in popularity. For students and workers, the abilty to type efficiently and accuracy is very usuful, because in the long term all those hours we've all spend typing away at a keyboard will be (and should be) minamilised. Picture this, if the average typing speed is around 40 words per minute (WPM), and you start practing a couple times a week, if you keep the habit up for (lets say) siz months, then over that time your typing speed could increase to over 100 words per minute. Thats over double the speed you were originally typing at. This then could save you half of the original time you normally would take to write those emails; Finish that essay; And literally everything else you use your keyboard for everyday.
